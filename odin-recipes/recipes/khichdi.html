Khicdi

Description

Khichdi is a simple healthy dish primarily popular is Western India.

Ingredients

  • Soaked black gram (sabut urad dal)
  • Rice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put Pressure cooker on stove
  2. Add soaked black gram
  3. Add Rice
  4. Add salt
  5. Let it COOK! till 3 whisteles from pressure cooker
  6. Enjoy! with curd or curry
